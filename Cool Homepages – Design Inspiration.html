About Us

Welcome to Cool Homepages, your number one source for website designs and templates. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best website templates, with a focus on design, speed, and price.

Founded in 1997 by Tim Donahue and later acquired by Karl Hindle, Cool Homepages has come a long way from its beginnings in the 90’s. When Karl Hindle first started out, his passion for website design drove him to relaunch the site so that Cool Homepages can offer you the best website designs and templates. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we’re able to turn our passion into our own website.

I hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
